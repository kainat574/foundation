Which is the biggest NGO in Pakistan?
Edhi Foundation is the biggest name in Pakistan's Non Government Organization. It is one of the best social welfare service providers across the world running on non-commercial, non-political and non-communal basis.
How many NGOs work in Pakistan
The number of active NGOs in the country is, at the very least, anywhere between 100,000 to 150,000, investigations by the Pakistan Centre for Philanthropy (PCP), a certification organisation for NGOs and charity institutions, reveal. By this count, there is at least one NGO for every 2,000 people.
Which is the biggest Foundation in the world?
