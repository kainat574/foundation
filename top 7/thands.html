RAMEEZA MUEEN

Founder Profile

Rameeza is a Chief Executive Officer at Transparent Hands, which provides free medical and surgical facililites to the deserving people of Pakistan. The organization also sets up free medical camps all over Pakistan. Rameeza has an expertise in business operations, development, leadership, management and tech industry. Rameeza has been recognized as a successful entrepreneur by Govt. of Pakistan, Women Chamber of Commerce, She Loves Tech and P@SHA. She is the recipient of “Tamgha-e-Imtiaz” – the 4th highest civilian award of Pakistan.

Prior to founding Transparent Hands, Rameeza has worked as an educationist in renowned institutes for 15 years. She is currently serving as a Founder and CEO of Transparent Hands Trust in Pakistan and Transparent Hands Foundation USA Inc. Through strategic foresight, drive and determination, she built a strong foundation for the development and growth of Transparent Hands not only in Pakistan but also in USA.

HISTORY OF TRANSPARENT HANDS

Transparent Hands is the largest crowdfunding platform in the healthcare sector of Pakistan registered with 2(36) tax exemptions. It offers a complete range of free healthcare services including medical and surgical treatments and free medical camps facilities to the underprivileged community of Pakistan. This online fundraising platform provides an online giving opportunity for donors from around the globe to donate online to needy patients in Pakistan while ensuring complete transparency. It also sets up free medical camps in the rural areas of Pakistan in which, free consultation, free medicines, free health awareness sessions, and free diagnostic tests facilities are also provided to deserving patients.

IMPACT ON PAKISTAN COMMUNITY

Transparent Hands is currently dealing with a number of surgeries including orthopedic, cardiac, neurology, gynecology, and other general surgeries. Cochlear Implant surgery for example cost around PKR 15 to 20 lakhs approximately $8,000 to $9,000. Transparent Hands provides it for free to its patients. It is also providing free medical procedures including Angioplasty, Hepatitis C Treatment,  Chemotherapy, MRI, Endoscopy, and Rehabilitation procedures for the disabled. Transparent Hands is focusing on reaching out to the masses throughout the country who don’t have access to basic healthcare facilities by setting up free medical camps.
